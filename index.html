    8. Data Collection

    The App records the following Data from your smartphone device:

  • GPS and location Data
  • Accelerometer Data
  • Gyroscope Data
  • Phone movement and screen activation Data
  • Time and date
  • App usage
  • Battery Level
    •

    The App uses the Data to calculate the following driving behaviors:

  • Speed
  • Acceleration
  • Hard Braking
  • Hard Cornering
  • Miles Driven
  • Phone Distraction
    •

    The Participating Driver will be assigned a Score determined by calculating the Data using an algorithm. The scoring criteria and algorithm are the sole property of CMT. Erie does not participate in assigning the Participating Driver’s Score.

    This Data is transmitted wirelessly via Wi-Fi or cellular data services to CMT. This Data is used to derive the Score. The Data is then sent from CMT to Erie. It is important to understand that all of the Data collected and transmitted is based on the Data collected by the App.

    10. Rewards

    Rewards are accumulated on a two-week basis. The two-week schedule is a predetermined schedule established by Erie that is not affected by the date of enrollment in YourTurn or the date a Participating Driver Registers. The two-week period can be located within the App. In order to earn rewards, a Participating Driver must meet all of the eligibility requirements per Section 5 and earn an Eligible Score. Rewards are awarded based upon the Participating Driver’s age, 50 tracked miles and the Participating Driver’s Score during the two-week period. At the end of the two-week period, the Participating Driver’s Score will be reset.

    The Participating Driver can access the dashboard within the App to receive feedback on driving and view Scores received for trips. While a Score will be assigned for each individual criterion, the overall Score is the Score used for rewards eligibility. Rewards will be awarded within forty-eight hours after the end of the two-week period. Rewards earned by a Participating Driver may be accumulated to redeem higher value gift cards available for Redemption through the App. Reward amounts are located within the App and are subject to change at the sole discretion of Erie. If the Eligible Score or reward amounts change, the Participating Driver will be notified of the changes on the App. Continued participation and use of the App will constitute the Participating Driver’s acceptance of the changed Eligible Score and reward amounts.

    The selection of available gift cards may vary and is subject to the Terms and Conditions established by the gift card vendor, Tango. You agree that Erie is not responsible for selecting the gift card vendor and agree to hold Erie harmless for all aspects of the App as related to Redemption, including but not limited to, the availability of gift cards, card values, problems with redeeming gift cards or reward points applicable to gift card Redemption.

    Any challenges to a trip must be made within 24 hours of the trip by contacting Erie Customer Care at 1-800-458-0811. The validity of a challenge will be determined solely by Erie. You agree to hold Erie harmless for challenge determinations. If a challenge is successful and results in an increase in your rewards issued for the prior reward period, Erie will communicate the correction to CMT. Your modified rewards will be distributed to you at the end of the two-week period following the challenge determination.

    If you do not challenge a driving Score within the above time frame, you waive your right to challenge the trip and any resulting effect such challenge may have had on your Score or rewards eligibility.
